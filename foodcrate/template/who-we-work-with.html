Researchers

How do we work with Researchers?

We are committed to providing policy makers with data and evidence so they can gain insights into the challenges facing the food system and generate the most appropriate policy responses. As well as conducting our own research, we work closely with researchers and academics in organizations and universities in Ghana.

Civil Societies

How do we work with Civil Society?

As our food systems face multiple challenges, civil society organizations (CSOs) play a key role in searching for solutions to transition to a healthier, more sustainable food system. By joining efforts, we can challenge policy makers, businesses and consumers to think differently about the food system and catalyze action so urgently needed.
We create effective partnerships with a range of civil society actors, to coordinate and mobilize collective action to bring about lasting change.
