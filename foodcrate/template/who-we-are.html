About our work



Our Vision

Is a sustainable food system which delivers healthy food and wellbeing for all.





Our Values

Collaborative, creative and innovative, impactful food system.





Our Mission

Is changing business practice, making food affordable, and to ensure everyone can afford and access a healthy and sustainable diet.
We are a not-for-profit entrepreneur and use inventive ideas to catalyze and deliver fundamental change in the food system by building
and synthesizing strong evidence, shaping coalitions, harnessing citizens’ voices and driving progress with impactful communications. We continually identify new opportunities for action, and trial new levers for change.

Our work with celebrities

An influencer or celebrity can support our work in a variety of different ways, whether through social media support, acting as campaign spokesperson, or taking an even more serious role by becoming an ambassador.
